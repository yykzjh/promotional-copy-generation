# Promotional Copy Writing Guidelines

## Structure
- Title: Eye-catching, highlight core selling points
- Body: Bullet points, clear logic
- CTA: Clear call to action

## Word Count Suggestions
- Xiaohongshu: Title within 20 chars, body 500-800 chars
- Weibo: Within 140 chars, concise
- Douyin: Paired with video, copy can be brief

## Taboos
- Avoid absolute claims (best, first, only, etc.)
- Avoid false advertising, exaggerated efficacy
- Comply with Advertising Law requirements

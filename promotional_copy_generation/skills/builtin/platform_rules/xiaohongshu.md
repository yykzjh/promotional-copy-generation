# Xiaohongshu Platform Guidelines

## Copy Restrictions
- Title: within 20 characters
- Body: 500-800 characters recommended, can be segmented
- Hashtags: 3-5, format #topic_name#

## Style
- Authentic, recommendation-oriented
- Suitable for emoji accents
- Conversational, approachable

## Taboo Terms
- Platform-sensitive words must be avoided
- Avoid hard-sell wording
